## What it does

Track every meaningful change to project configuration with a clear audit trail and configurable alerts — no more "who changed the workflow?" detective work.

## Why teams use it

- **Detect drift instantly** when schemes, workflows, or fields change.
- **Trace back** with a full history of who changed what and when.
- **Stay compliant** with an evidence trail your auditors will love.
