## What it does

Astrolink renders the relationship graph between issues — blocks, is blocked by, parent, child, duplicates, and custom link types — as a navigable visualization right inside Jira.

## Why teams use it

- **Untangle dependencies** before sprint planning.
- **Find the blocker behind the blocker** in two clicks.
- **Communicate scope** to stakeholders without screenshots of issue lists.

## Built on Forge

Astrolink runs on Atlassian's Forge platform. That means data stays inside your Atlassian Cloud tenancy — no third-party servers, no extra DPA reviews.
