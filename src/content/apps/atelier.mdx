## What it does

A growing toolkit of Confluence macros for callouts, structured content, embeds, and information design — every component thoroughly accessibility-tested.

## Why teams use it

- **Pages that read well** without reaching for screenshots.
- **Consistent visual language** across spaces and authors.
- **Performance-first** — macros render fast even on long pages.
