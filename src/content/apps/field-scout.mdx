## What it does

Scan every custom field in your Jira Cloud tenancy: where it's used, who fills it in, and how often. Find duplicates, surface candidates for retirement, and clean up with confidence.

## Why teams use it

- **Faster Jira** — fewer fields means faster screens, faster search, lighter exports.
- **Migration-ready** — a clean field inventory is the prerequisite to any major Jira change.
- **Safe cleanup** — see exactly what's affected before you remove anything.
